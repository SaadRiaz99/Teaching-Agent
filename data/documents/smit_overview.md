# Sikkim Manipal Institute of Technology (SMIT) Overview

## About SMIT
Sikkim Manipal Institute of Technology (SMIT) is a constituent college of Sikkim Manipal University (SMU). Established in 1997, SMIT is located in Rangpo, Sikkim. The institute offers various Undergraduate and Postgraduate programs in Engineering, Science, and Management.

## Academic Programs
- Undergraduate: B.Tech (4 years), B.Sc (3 years), BBA (3 years), BCA (3 years)
- Postgraduate: M.Tech (2 years), M.Sc (2 years), MBA (2 years), MCA (2 years)
- Doctoral: Ph.D. in all disciplines
- Lateral Entry: Direct admission to 2nd year B.Tech for diploma holders

## Training and Placement
The Training and Placement (T&P) Cell at SMIT facilitates placement opportunities for all graduating students. It connects students with industry stakeholders, corporations, academic and research institutions. The T&P Cell conducts employability training sessions through collaboration with reputable training organizations.
